Horchata

Horchata is a creamy chilled drink made from rice with cinnamon. There are many other optional add-ins. Some variations are nuts, vanilla, fruit, other spices. Flowers can be added such as hibiscus or mexican marigolds.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Cinnamon

Directions

  1. Boil water, spices, and optional ingredients if you are using.
  2. Use the hot water to soak the rice for 24 hours, more or less.
  3. Blend in the blender.
  4. Strain through a cloth or mesh.
  5. refrigerate or chill over ice and enjoy.
