Spicy Sushi Mayo

The light orange creamy delicous sauce that you often see drizzled over sushi rolls.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Mayonaise
  • 2 TB Siracha

Directions

  1. Mix well.
  2. Can be placed in a condiment squirt style bottle to be drizzled across sushi.
  3. Refrigerate and label with use-by date the same as the mayo.
